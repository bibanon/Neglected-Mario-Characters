Nogamis T.

Created By: Bob Grocer
Name: Nogamis T.
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Toad Town
Friends: Merlon
Enemies: Kamek
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Loner
Description of looks: Nogamis is a normal toad who likes to wear a white cloak instead of traditional toad clothing. He always carries a metal rod.
Short Description of Life: After Nogamis left his home to make a living for himself, he went to Merlon's house and begged to be taught the ways of the magicians. Merlon taught Nogamis for ten years, and then pronounced Nogamis as his own master. Nogamis then hiked up to Shooting Star Summit in order to find inspiration. He was met by Kamek, who was also training up there. Kamek beat Nogamis down very badly that day, and said some very bad things about his mother. Nogamis vowed to kill Kamek whatever it took from that day forth.
