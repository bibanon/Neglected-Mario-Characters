Name: The Dark-Avenge-Puff
Gender: Male
Birthplace: A temple deep in the Whispy Woods
Friends: All good-hearted dreampuffs
Enemies: Nightmares and Dark Matter
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Good
Description of looks: White Dreampuff with a black helmet like Samus's and black cape that is enbroded with a golden star and a star-gun attached to his right arm. He has the ability to fly higher and farther than an average dreampuff. He also has a white goatee and has Maximum Tomato Grenades. He rides a motorcycle with star wheels and a Pep-Soda oil spil ability.
Short Description of Life: When the powers of the Star and Heart Rod joined together in the Temple of the Stars he is born. He lives to protect popstar and stop the Dark Matter and Nightmares.
Kirby Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): A lot o' Kirby games and Kirby 64 as a helper.
Currently: Resting in the Temple of the Stars, waiting to be called upon.
