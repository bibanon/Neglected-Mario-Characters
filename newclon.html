Cloner McCloner

Created By: Davey Oshi
Name: Cloner McCloner
Gender: Who knows! (An it?)
Birthplace: Kemak’s Secret Underground Lab
Friends: Kemak, Bowser, and all clones
Enemies: CHEESE! And Toad
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Evil
Description of looks: Looks like a blue typewriter with the power to create clones.
Short Description of Life: After being beaten by Yoshi, Kemak created the ultimate plan of destruction. This plan now plays a huge role in the Mushroom Kingdom... kidnapping the princess! So he created Cloner. Cloner captured the princess, then cloned an army of Bowsers and together they captured Mario and Luigi. When Cloner was destroying them Toad appeared out of nowhere and tried to save Mario and the gang and nearly failed. Toad forgot his mushrooms and he did bring was a chunk of old, smelly cheese. Crazily, he ate it. Suddenly, he gained super strength and clobbered Cloner and his army. Ahhhh! The power of cheese!!!
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): SMW 3: Toad to the rescue, Super Mario Kart
Currently: There have been sightings of him in a dump.
