Reswob Apook

Created By: Roger North
Name: Reswob Apook
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Koopa Laboratory
Friends: Mario, Luigi, Princess Toadstool, Toad, Yoshi, Etc.
Enemies: Bowser Koopa, The Koopalings, Wart, Kamek, Etc.
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Good
Description of looks: He looks like Bowser Except that His Skin is Blue and His Hair is Green and His Belly is Purple.
Short Description of Life: Bowser wanted Someone to understand him so He cloned himself. Unfortunately Reswob turned out to be good instead of Evil Which Made Bowser so upset that He wanted to Lock him in the Dungeon But Reswob Escaped and Met up with Mario and his friends and wanted to join their team. At first They thought He was Bowser But He managed to convince Them He wasn't and They accepted him as a friend of theirs. He even helps them defeat Bowser from time to time.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Super Mario Bros. Super Mario Bros: The Lost Levels Super Mario Bros.3 Super Mario World Super Mario RPG Super Mario 64 and The Upcoming Super Mario Adventure
Currently: Helping Mario save Princess Toadstool From Bowser.
Other Comments: I thought it would be cool for Bowser to have a good counterpart who is on Mario's side.
