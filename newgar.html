Gar

Created By: Greg Records
Name: Gar
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Popstar
Friends: Kirby, Mario, Luigi, and Dk
Enemies: Wart and Smithy
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Loner
Description of looks: Looks like Luigi without a mustache or hat. Wears an orange vest and loose gray pants.
Short Description of Life: After studying all of Kirby and Mario's movies Gar set on a quest to be a hero like them. But he needed cash and enlisted in tournaments for cash. He currently has mastered his whirl technique (up and B) and is now trying to create more techniques and enter in a Death match (for money and fame of course).
