Clumsy Koopa

Created By: RED
Name: Clumsy Koopa
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Koopa Village
Friends: Mario, Luigi, etc.
Enemies: Bowser, the Turtle Tribe, and other threats to the Mushroom Kingdom
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Good
Description of looks: He looks like the Koopa Troopas, except his shell is in a rainbow of colors, but still friendly.
Short Description of Life: He likes to be helpful, but like the name says, he's all thumbs, and sometimes gets things right.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Paper Mario, Mario Party 3
Currently: Peach's Castle
Other Comments: He has good attacks, fine stamina, but usually he misses his attacks, but he never hits his partner (except the odds of that are 1 in 50). He's not a good dancer, but sings fine, but all Koopa Troopas in Koopa Village still respect him (including Kooper). He's scared of ghosts, especially Boos, VERY shy of females, not too scared of the dark, but when it comes to swimming, he's top-notch, so he never screws up when swimming. Mario will need to carry some X Accuracy along so Clumsy Koopa and attack straight. Cons: Clumsiness, when he gets confused, he can sometimes strike his own partner by mistake (or himself), he's quite slow, and when he helps, he trips on his own two feet. Pros: He is a good partner, his swimming is top-notch, and in Mario Party 3, since his low pay is a total plus, players only need to pay one coin, making his Attack: 2, Stamina: 3, and Salary: 1.
