These are the games where Toad has had an active role. Not much, is it?

  • Mario Kart/Mario Kart64- He was a pretty good driver, with THE BEST handling.

Mario Games he's had a major role in the story:
Although Toad has not been in very many active roles, in most games he has had a large part in the story.
  • SMB- The final mushroom in World 7 was Toad. All the others were related to him
  • SMB:3- Toad's Mushroom House o' Goodies! Also the card game and such
  • Super Mario RPG-Toad had a HUGE story line role in this. Besides being a beginner tutor, he was VERY helpful throughout the game.
  • SM64- Gives you some free stars and helps with the game.

Forgotten Heroism:
Info. about Toad and reasons he has been neglected.
  • He runs around Mushroom Kingdom/World to help Mario throughout his quest. Shouldn't Mario, the great hero, be able to get along by himself?
  • He had a decent character in both SMB:2, dream Tennis, and Mario Kart. Fast, agile, nimble, are these the character traits of a hero or what?
  • He's one of the higher ranking Mushroom officials on the entire Mushroom Kingdom. Pretty cool job if you ask me.
  • He usually supplies Mario with Items though out the game. The net time you use a Super Leaf in SMB:3, remember where you got it.
  • Anyone who's ever read the Mario comics or books knows it's Toad that always saves everyones butt.
