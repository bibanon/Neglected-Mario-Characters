Zenobia

Created By: Roger North
Name: Zenobia
Gender: Female
Birthplace: Yoshi's Island
Friends: Mario, Luigi, Princess Peach Toadstool, Princess Daisy, Toad, Yoshi, Mallow,Geno, Etc.
Enemies: King Bowser Koopa, The Koopalings, Wart, Kamek, Tatanga, Wario, Waluigi, Smithy,, Etc.
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral:
Description of looks: She has a long blonde hair and blue eyes and She often wears short shorts and a tank top.
Short Description of Life: She was supposed to be taken to Hyrule as a baby but Kamek kidnapped her. Yoshi who was also a prisoner of Kamek at the time took her with him and abandoned him on the doorstep of Toad's Parents. They sent her to a warp zone in the Real World Where She later met Mario and Luigi. She later went back to The Mushroom World with them and has constantly tagged along with them on their adventures.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Super Mario Bros. Currently: Helping Mario save Princess Toadstool.
Other Comments: I thought Mario and Luigi could use a female hero on their side.
