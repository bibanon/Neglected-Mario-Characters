Kyte Crashalot

Created By: David "Booster" King
Name: Kyte Crashalot
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Somewhere in the the middle of nowhere.
Friends: Well...I think I saw Bill and him in a bar once.
Enemies: Dr. Freakenstien, Sir Evilaff, any Nintendo dude you can think of. Mostly James Bond
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Natural
Description of looks: He's drawn anime style. He has tan skin, red eyes, red hair, red T-shirt with yellow star on front, leather gloves, baggy brown shorts, yellow shoes.
Short Description of Life: Kyte wanted to be a video game star of secret agent games. He tried to join up with Nintendo, claiming his game has lots of action and flight simulation. They chose James Bond instead. Since then, he's held a grudge against 007. A little while later, he tried to become a star of a Mario game and almost succeeded...until he accidently crashed his helicopter, Underdog, into Mario's house. He was soon kicked out of the the video business for good.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Super Smash bros, Mario Party 2, The Adventures of Kyte, The Adventures of Kyte 2. Freakenstien's revenge
Currently: Living in a trailer park.
