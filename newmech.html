Mecha-Luigi

Created By: Adam Neikirk
Name: Mecha-Luigi
Gender: None
Birthplace: Mushroom Kingdom Labs
Friends: Mario, Luigi, Peach etc.
Enemies: Turtle Tribe and/or other threats to MushroomLand
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Good
Description of looks: Looks like Lugi only made of titanium and has a red L emroided on chest and titanium cap.
Short Description of Life: Mecha-Luigi was created by in Mushroom Labs for the sole purpose of protecting Mushroom Kingdom when the Brothers don't feel like it. It is very loyal and hates the Turtle Tribe.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): M64 and Super Smash Brothers (as a secret)
Currently: Patrolling Peach's Castle
Other Comments: Powers that it has: The abilty to heal itself and others, the ability to shoot lasers out of it's eyes and shoot giant fireballs out of it's chest. It can also fly into the air using rocket boots. Problems that it has: Malfunctions when it hears the word 'Tuba' and sometimes short circuits when it gets hit in the nose.
