Doomclock

Created By: Bob Grocer
Name: Doomclock
Gender: Unknown
Birthplace: The Mushroom Kingdom
Friends: Enemies of Mario
Enemies: Mario or anyone taking the place of Mario
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Evil
Description of looks: It is normally a three digit number that likes to count down from a high number. Once it appears to be zero, all heroes in the area die and must regenerate with extra lives.
Short Description of Life: It woke up...and then began counting. When it reached zero, it started over again. The cycle of doom began.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): All of them except for Yoshi's Island, Super Mario Kart, Mario Kart 64, Mario Party, Mario Tennis...and other games that Doomclock is not on.
Currently: Ticking down to zero.
Other Comments: "There was a great disturbace in time, as if a lot of heroes in the Mushroom Kingdom cried out at once...and then were silenced."
