Meowco

Created By: Roger North
Name: Meowco
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Plantet Catatania
Friends: Mario, Luigi, Princess Toadstool, Toad, Yoshi, Mallow and Geno
Enemies: Bowser Koopa, The Koopalings, Wart, Kamek, Smithy, and all other bad guys from the Mario Universe
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: N/A
Description of looks: He is a Cat Like Alien Dressed in a White Shirt Blue Pants and Red Shoes
Short Description of Life: He Came to Mushlandia Because His Planet Catatania Exploded. At First The Yoshies hated him so Bowser decided to have him work for him. So Bowser Promised him he would help him fix his Spaceship if He kidnapped Princess Toadstool for him. So He kidnaps Princess Toadstool for Bowser But Bowser has him thrown out of the Castle and from then on He knew Bowser wasn't going to keep his Promise So Meowco wanted revenge. He even helped Mario and Luigi save Princess Toadstool and After That They go back to Yoshi's Island only to find out That The Yoshies pushed the spaceship into the Water. But When Mario told the Yoshies that He helped them save Princess Toadstool They apologized and He forgave Them. He even moves into Yoshi's House with Him and They Become Roomates. Ever since Then He Joins Mario and his friends on Their Adventures.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Super Mario Bros.1-3, Super Mario World, Super Mario World 2:Yoshi's Island, Super Mario RPG, Super Mario 64, Yoshi's Story, The Adventures of Meowco, and Mario's Friends
