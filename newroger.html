Roshi

Created By: Roger North
Name: Roshi
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Yoshi's Island
Friends: Yoshi, Mario, Luigi, Princess Toadstool, Toad, Mallow, Geno
Enemies: Bowser, The Koopalings, Kamek, Boshi, Etc.
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Good
Description of looks: Looks Like a Red Yoshi
Short Description of Life: Roshi is Basically Yoshi's Twin Brother. He feels Neglected to Yoshi like Luigi does to Mario. In Fact Roshi would make a good confidant for Luigi.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Super Mario World, Super Mario World 2: Yoshi's Island, Super Mario RPG, Yoshi's Story, Super Smash Bros. Luigi's Quest and The Upcoming Super Mario Adventure.
Currently: Trying to follow in Yoshi's Footsteps
Other Comments: Roshi is to Yoshi as Luigi is to Mario.
