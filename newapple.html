Princess Apple

Created By: UltraCinder64
Name: Princess Apple
Gender: Female
Birthplace: Mushroom Kingdom
Friends: Everybody from the Mario group (Mario, Luigi, Peach, etc.)
Enemies: Bowser and the Koopa Troopas
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Good
Description of looks: Looks like Peach but has brown hair and a red dress
Short Description of Life: She was an ordinary girl who lived with her parents and older sister in the Mushroom Kingdom. One day at the age of 14, while vacationing on Giant Land, Apple discovered a magical item in the plains known as the Ultra Mushroom. She touched it, and her family was amazed when Apple suddenly became a giantess. With her new size, she was made queen of Giant Land, and took her family in to her new home.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Super Smash Bros. 2 (as a hidden character) and Apple's Quest
Currently: As queen, Apple has to live in Giant Land, but she visits her friends at Peach's Castle from time to time.
Other Comments: None.
