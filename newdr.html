Dr. Mushroom

Created By: Luigi423
Name: Dr. Mushroom
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Mushroom Village
Friends: Mario, Luigi, Peach, Yoshi
Enemies: All of Mario's enemies and Toad
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Good
Description of looks: He's basically Toad with a labcoat and glasses.
Short Description of Life: Dr. Mushroom and Toad are twins. He feels neglected to Toad, sort of Luigi does to Mario. He thinks Peach likes Toad, but Doc also likes. Doc is also the creator of all the items, but the mushroom people don't believe him about that.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Probably all of them. After all, he did create all the items.
Currently: Inventing new items for future Mario games.
Other Comments: I created Dr. Mushroom so Mario could have a scientist in his games. He is my only created character so far.
