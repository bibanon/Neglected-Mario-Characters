Zeed the MagiKoopa

Created By: Adam Neikirk
Name: Zeed the MagiKoopa
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Bowser's Castle Magic Pool
Friends: None
Enemies: None
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Loner
Description of looks: Looks like Kamek but wears a navy-blue robe and has a gnarled walking stick. Has a long beard with a tint of blue and streaks of black.
Short Description of Life: Zeed was born a MagiKoopa, but didn't like the slow moving ways of the Turtle Tribe. Zeed got away and now lives in Snow World. Sometimes he'll come out to give advice to weary travellers but then heads back into his cave to work in a junky lab where he cunducts mysterious experimants. Sometimes you can see his cave lighting up with color. Few people ever find him so he remains hidden in his cave that rests high up on the highest ridge. He is thirty times more powerful than Kamek.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Search for Zeed or Luigi's quest for the secret of Snow World
Currently: Trying to turn Dark Matter into apple juice.
Other Comments: If you dream it, he will come....
