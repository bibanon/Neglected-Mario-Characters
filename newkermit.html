Kermit the Hermit

Created By: Jeremy McBride
Name: Kermit the Hermit
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Tadpole Pond
Friends: Anyone that is not famous
Enemies: Anyone that is famous
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Loner
Description of looks: 99.9% similar to Kermit the Frog
Short Description of Life: Kermit the Hermit and Kermit the Frog were born as twins in Tadpole Pond. As they got older, Kermit the Frog went into showbusiness. Kermit the Hermit wanted to become famous, like his brother, but in video games. Since he enjoyed Mario games the most, he tried to get in one. He failed, terribly. This led him to become a homicidal maniac. One day, he learned of a club called Neglected Mario Characters. He decided to join and devote his life as a homicidal maniac to the destruction of all people famous.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Super Mario RPG; Super Mario 64
Currently: Mercilessly torturing his twin brother, Kermit the Frog
Other Comments: To this day nobody knows where he lives. Just hope you don’t find it and happen to be famous.
