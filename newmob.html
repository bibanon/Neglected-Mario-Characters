"Maniacal" Gary Koopa

Created By: Mobius Destroyer
Name: "Maniacal" Gary Koopa
Gender: Male
Birthplace: The Bermuda Trianclaw
Friends: Evil guys (Bowser, Clawdia, Koopa Kids, etc) and strangely Toad
Enemies: Good guys (Mario, Luigi, Peach, etc) except Toad
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Call him whatever you want
Description of looks: Iggy's hair, Ludwig's build, gun belt, blue vest, single spike shell, and other non-available Koopa items like a cap

Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): SMB3, SMW, Super Mario in Koopa Land, Life on Koopa Kourt, Super Koopa RPG, Gary in Gary's Land
Currently: Living with his girlfriend, Daisy.
Other Comments: Gary is the only Koopa kid that can ride his own shell.
