Mushroom X

Created By: Roger North
Name: Mushroom X
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Somewhere in The Mushroom Kingdom
Friends: The Good Guys and The Bad Guys
Enemies: The Bad Guys and The Good Guys
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Loner
Description of looks: He looks like Toad except with Sunglasses and a Black Jacket
Short Description of Life: Mushroom X is a Double Agent who works for Both The Mushroom Kingdom and the Koopa Kingdom. He constantly switches sides to see who pays more money.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Super Mario Bros.3 Super Mario World Super Mario RPG Super Mario 64 and The upcoming Super Mario Adventure
Currently: Trying to decide to work for Princess Toadstool or Bowser Koopa.
Other Comments: I think that Mario should have a Double Agent in his Games.
