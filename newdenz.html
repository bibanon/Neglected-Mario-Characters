Denzenello

Created By: Joseph Miller
Name: Denzenello
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Earth on the Gaian dimension ( He refuses to give an exact place)
Friends: King Bowser and the Magikoopas
Enemies: Mario and Luigi
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Evil
Description of looks: Denzenello is skinny but his body is not quite as weak as one would think (Bowser's trying to get him to to work out more often). His hair is long and somewhat wavy. His eyes are a startlingly bright brown( Almost a tannish hue). Wears a pair of dark black pants, Black boots, Navy blue shirt, and and a black trenchcoat.
Short Description of Life: There in a reason that Bowser has Magick users(the Magikoopas) in his army. This story begins with a tear in the fabric of Timespace, similar to the one that brought the Marios to the Mushroom world. A young boy was hurtled through this rip, which connected the Gaian Dimension (Where most humans exist) and the Mushroom Dimension. This boy was Dennis Zendell, a teenager with powerful magick. Dennis ended up on Dinosaur World ( Where the Yoshies live) at about the time that King Bowser was building his troops on the small planet, preparing for an interplanetary war with the Mushroom world. Dennis was found unconscious by Bowser's forces and taken to the main castle. He was then put into a dungeon cell where he waited for interrogation, by Bowser himself. When he finally woke up, he was quite scared (Just the way Bowser likes his victims), but he quickly formed a plan. The following discussion ensued:
Dennis : Where am I? Who are you?
Bowser : I WILL ASK THE QUESTIONS HERE!!!!!! What is your name and how did you get to the dinosaur world?
Dennis :............. You may call me........ Denzenello. I came here, not of my own will by way of some sort of rip in the fabric of timespace. This is " Dinosaur world " ?
Bowser : Yes, I just said that.
Denzenello : Are you by any chance the one known as King Bowser?
Bowser : ?!?........... How did you know that?
Denzenello : Is there a mushroom world with two pesky plumber and a beautiful princess?
Bowser : How have you learned all of this? Are you a spy of some sort?
Denzenello : Oh no no no....... I have seen your battle with the plumbers and I know just how bad you have been beaten by them.
Bowser : I wasn't beaten THAT bad.....
Denzenello : Yes of course............. Perhaps what you need is a new type of soldier.
Bowser : Why I oughtta..............? What type of soldier?
Denzenello : A soldier that uses magick. A magikoopa.
Bowser : Hmm...... I like the idea. How will we go about training these soldiers.
Denzenello : Leave that to me I am a powerful mage and I am able to teach the art of magick to some of your troops. It will be a long strenuous task and success, for most is unlikely but I will do my best to serve you. I, too, wish to finally defeat those bothersome plumbers.
Thus Dennis, now called Denzenello, fights alongside Bowser in a heated attempt to finally defeat Bowser and control the world. Though Denzenello says that he's loyal to Bowser, his actions are still unclear and it is often difficult to determine who's side he's really on.
