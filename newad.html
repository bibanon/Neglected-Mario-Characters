Adelé Monstro

Created By: peach@pearland.com (Peach)
Name: Adelé Monstro
Gender: Female
Birthplace: Monstro Town, Mushlandia
Friends: Mario, Luigi, Peach, Mallow, Geno, etc....
Enemies: Smithy, his gang, and all the other masters from SMRPG
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Good
Description of looks: Adelé is a mushroom person. She's taller than Toad. She wears a lavender dress, white apron with green, brown, and red trim, and a shawl to match. Her light brown hair is often braided into a single braid in the back.
Short Description of Life: Adelé is an orphan, rasied by her grandmother, whom most people call 'Monstermama'. Right before Exor made his appearance in Bowser's Keep, Monstermama sent Adelé to Mushroom Kingdom to buy supplies. Well, soon after Adelé got there, her locket was stolen by Croco, and Mario and Mallow help her get it back...
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Super Mario RPG (I made her up for that game only, but she'll fit in others.) Super mario Kart, Mario Kart 64, Mario Party, Mario Golf
