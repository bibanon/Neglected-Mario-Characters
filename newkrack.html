Prince Krack

Created By: Halken
Name: Prince Krack
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Mecheye, like Kirby's foe Meta Knight
Friends: Mario, Luigi, Peach, Toad, Yoshi, DK (the good one), Wario and other good guys
Enemies: Bowser, Phone the clone, Fred the spanyard, Smithy and otherbad guys
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Good
Description of looks: A Kirby-sized knight. He wears a purple cape and carries a glowing sword. He carefully protects his hypercute Dream Lander face with a mask. He looks just like the Meta Knight character from Kirby's Adventure and Kirby Super Star.
Short Description of Life: Krack the Knight was born and raised on a planet called Mecheye. He was really great and he won lots of prizes. Then, he left and found a new place to live on: Mushania. He made friends with Mario and his gang, and fought bad guys like Smithy. He decided tostay in Mushania.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Super Mario RPG, Yoshi's Story, Mario 64, Mario Party, Super SmashBros.
Currently: Living in Mushania.
Other Comments: As a matter of fact, Krack the Knight is a charactermade by the maker of Kirby. All I did was given him a cool name.
