Malek/Morpheous

Created By: King Calamari
Name: Malek/Morpheous
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Koopahari Desert
Friends: Kamek, his loyal troops, Sub-Conians
Enemies: Mario, Peach, Bowser, and all who oppose him
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Formerly good, currently evil
Description of looks: Blue shelled Koopa Troopa, flowing blue cape, large spearlike weapon, koopa skull as mask, bright blue eyes
Short Description of Life: He was born immediately after the first Mushroom War. He was an orphan, taken in by Peach, and trained by Mario. He was named Malek. He became a great warrior. When he was segregated by the mushroom people, though, he joined Bowser. In Bowser's army he had toxic waste spilled on him. It mutated him, giving him the ability to change his shape, or "morph". That's where he got the name Morpheous. Although he had great powers, the koopas treated him like a freak. This caused him to defect from Bowser's army and create his own army to wipe out the koopas and the mushroom people.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Mario RPG, Mario Bros. 2, Super Smash Bros., Legacy of the Koopa, Super Mario Bros.: The Final Chapter?
Currently: Plotting to kill Bowser, Mario, and Peach
Other Comments: He has the power to shoot lightning bolts, turn invisible, and create dry bones's & boos. His army is made up of the same enemies in Wart's old army(Shy Guys, Bob-Ombs, Triclyde, etc.)
