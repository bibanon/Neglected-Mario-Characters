Luibi

Created By: N64 Chick
Name: Luibi (pronuned loo-ee-bee)
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Unknown
Friends: Luigi, Baby Mario, Paratroopa
Enemies: Waluigi, Kamak, Wario
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Good
Description of looks: A baby Luigi with large paratroopa-like wings. A skilled flyer and a quick runner.
Short Description of Life: Mostly hangs around with his friends who teach him how to run and fly. Also fights minor foes like goombas and shyguys.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Super Mario RPG and Mario Tennis
Currently: Trying to learn Hyper Burn and evolve (grow) into Luiki (the little kid stage)
Other Comments: As Luibi grows, the wings shrink so by the time he reaches the Luiti (teen) stage the wings are almost gone. This might explain why Luigi has no wings what-so-ever.
