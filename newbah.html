Bahumut

Created By: HairyHomer
Name: Bahumut
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Unknown
Friends: Luigi, Link, Ness, Mario, Peach
Enemies: Bowser, The Koopa Kids, anything in Bowser's army, Toad (Bahumut often tries to eat him)
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Good
Description of looks: Bahumut's looks are hard to describe. Bahumut LOVES jewelry. It's part of his species's culture that even the males wear jewelry.
Short Description of Life: Bahumut is often confused for a Pokemon. He's not one. When he and Luigi met, he tried to turn Luigi into a frog with a magical staff he carries around. It failed because Ness (a good friend of Luigi's who was with him at the time) used PSI to reflect the spell back at Bahumut (The spell wore off in about an hour). Now, Bahumut hangs around the castle stealing Peach's necklaces.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): None really. Bahumut would fit into the show, however.
