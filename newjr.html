Luigi Mario jr

Created By: Adam Neikirk
Name: Luigi Mario jr
Gender: Male
Birthplace: MushroomLand
Friends: Mario, Luigi (his father), Peach, Daisy (his mother), Toad
Enemies: Bowser, Wario, and all other bad guys in MushroomLand
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Good
