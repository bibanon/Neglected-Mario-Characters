Yoshi

Mario Games he has been in:
  • SMW- First appearance! Mario rode him through levels, ate stuff, and added humor AND fun to the game.
  • SMW2:YI- STAR ROLE!!! Saved Mario from baby Bowser and Kamek. Beautiful graphics. I enjoy them much more than Mario 64. (<----controversy time -Kyle)
  • Yoshi- An OK puzzle game. The first Yoshi appearance on NES!!!
  • Yoshi's Cookie- I don't know, I never played it. Heard it wasn't good though. Has almost nothing to do with Yoshi, just a cameo . Also a puzzle game.
  • Tetris Attack- Also has little to do with Yoshi. Based on pictures from YI. Never played it. A third puzzle game for the dino. Sheesh.
  • Yoshi's Safari- A pitiful attempt by Nintendo to increase Super Scope sales. Never played it.
  • SM64- A SMALL CAMEO!!! THAT'S IT!!! WHY WOULD ANYONE NEED 100 LIVES AFTER THEY HAVE ALL THE STARS!!! THIS IS WHY I DO NOT HAVE A N64 YET!!! NO LUIGI, NOT MUCH YOSHI!!! BAD, BAD, NINTENDO!!! A major disappointment for all neglected char. fans.
