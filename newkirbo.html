Kuribo

Created By: ostro64@hotmail.com
Name: Kuribo
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Giant World-later moved to Sky World
Friends: No one. He's a mean guy.
Enemies: Goombas
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Evil
Description of looks: A giant guy with three legs and magic shoes (he can walk on anything, even Spiny). He wears green clothes.He's short (for a giant), but stocky and has a big, stupid grin on his fat face. He wears a giant toupee to cover his baldness.
Short Description of Life: He was born in Giant World and was unhappy that everyone was bigger than him. He craved power. So he moved to Sky World, where everyone was smaller than him. He became tyrant ruler and crushed anyone who was in his way. One day, though, a band of renegade Goombas and their leader, Goomba the quick, snuck into his Spiral Tower while he was sleeping. They made their way to his palace at the top and took his three giant shoes (Bowser wanted them). Kuribo suddenly awakened screaming from his bad dream about Shyguys and giant frogs. He spotted the Goombas and went after them. They ran for their lives and were about to get in the pipe to get to the ground, when one weak Goomba (weaker than the rest) tripped and fell. Kuribo stepped on the Goomba and slipped on its Goomba mush. He went tumbling over all of the Goombas except Goomba the Quick, who had escaped with his mad speed. Kuribo continued to slide on the Goomba mush until he slid off the edge of the clouds, falling to his doom. His shoes were taken to a factory by Goomba the Quick, where Bowser turned them into 3 mechanical shoes for his Goomba army.
