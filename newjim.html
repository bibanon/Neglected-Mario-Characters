Jim

Created By: FredFan
Name: Jim the Shakey Guy
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Unexplored jungle on Yoshi's Island
Friends: Bill the Extra Guy and Fred the Spanyard
Enemies: All who hate salt, anybody he chooses to be
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Neutral
Description of looks: A somewhat gold and orange shy guy. You can pick him out of other shys by looking for a salt shaker. He is a salt addict; if you saw his x-ray, you would see the clogged arteries. Don't ask me how he lives.
Short Description of Life: Jim lived with his family on a remote corner of Yoshi's Island. He was a quiet moron, until that day when he mistakingly grabbed a salt shaker insted of his lunch thermos and took a swig. A new world opened up to him, and he was addicted to salt from childhood to now. He always carries salt shakers; his "friends" giving him the name "Shakey Guy"
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Super Mario Kitchen, Salt Quest 64
