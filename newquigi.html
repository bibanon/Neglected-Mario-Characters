Short Description of Life: Amazingly enough, Quigi was born on popstar. he was adopted and raised by King DeDeDe. he later then moved to the Mushroom Kingdom, where he later met up with Luigi. the two became instant enemies and fought hard. however,out of no where, a bob-omb appeared, and blew them both away. Luigi landed safely in the mushroom kingdom, but Quigi wasn't that fortunate and landed in the barrel volcano. he was assumed dead.......until years later when he was spotted fighting in the Mushroom Wars.
