Created By: J. Mycroft
Name: *&%#$!, aka Muerto
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Comes from Curse Canyon, appears first in Mildew Town
Friends: None, but he pretends to like Wario, Waluigi, Syrup, Wallow, Pitt, etc.
Enemies: Geno
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Very Evil
Description of looks: A possessed doll. Wears a black cape, and a black cap with a skull on it. Wood is darker then Geno's. Eyes are a burning red, and hair is purple. Shoes are gray and pointy. Shoots poison darts out of his hands.
Short Description of Life: A spirit who comes from Curse Canyon, and possesses a doll. Assisted Wario and Waluigi in defeating La Forge, but whose side is he really on? Actually, he wants to spread hate and destruction, and wants to make the Washroom Kingom his personal shrine.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Wario RPG
Currently: Unknown
Other Comments: Is Geno's evil twin. I named him Muerto because that is the Italian "Latin" word for "Death," and "Geno" is the Italian (Latin) word for "life." Getting better, eh? Quote: "I'll GET YOU, Geno!!!"
