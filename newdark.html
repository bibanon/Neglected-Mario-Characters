Name: The Dark-Puff
Gender: Unknown
Birthplace: Unknown
Friends: Dark Matter and other evil life forms
Enemies: Kirby
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Evil
Description of looks: It looks like Kirby, except black instead of pink. It has a single red eye like Dark Matter. It has two shoulder pads, like the Meta-Knight does, except his are white and gold rimmed. It also has a red cape.
Short Description of Life: Dark Matter created the Dark-Puff so that part of Dark Matter could be in the form of a Dreamlander. This was another attempt to destroy Kirby. However, the Dark-Puff took over and can now control Dark Matter.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Any Kirby games with Dark Matter in it. The Dark-Puff would be the final form of Dark Matter that Kirby would have to fight.
