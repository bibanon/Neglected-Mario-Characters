Mushroom Koopa

Created By: Sonic the Evil
Name: Mushroom Koopa
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Inside the mouth of one of those fire breathing Bowser statues in Bowser's Castle.
Friends: Luigi, Yoshi, Toad (Best), Baby Luigi, Birdo, Boo, Daisy, Toadette.
Enemies: Mario even though he works with him, Bowser, Bowser JR, Wart (arch enemy), Koopa Kid and Koopa Pack.
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Good
Description of looks: He looks like a winged Koopa Troopa but his shell is spotted red and white (a color fills each little square section), he has black gloves with spikes around the rims, white shoes and is always on his red skateboard. He has the following attacks:
Skate Smack
Speed Boarder
Surf, Skate and Slide
Shredding Gloves
Short Description of Life: Mushroom is only 10-years old and was at first a member of Bowser's army. He worked his way up to top member by defeating Mario and co. over and over. But he was fired when Bowser JR got mad at him for being his dad's favorite. Bowser JR transformed into a mario look-a-like and defeated him. He was then thrown into the mushrooms dungeon where he dreamed he was in subcon saving Toad's skin left and right. The next day Toad demanded he was let out of the prison. He currently fights alongside Mario, even though they still hate each other, in the war agains King Bowser Koopa.
