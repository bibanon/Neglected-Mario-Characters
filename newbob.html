Bob the Beezo

Created By: Yoshinator
Name: Bob the Beezo
Gender: Male
Birthplace: Frog City, Subcon
Friends: All who have ever felt neglected, left out, or under-appreciated. Oh, that and the working man.
Enemies: Mario, Bowser, and other bureaucrats
Good / Evil / Loner / Neutral: Good
Description of looks: Just a beezo
Short Description of Life: He was born in poverty, but, through hard work, managed to make a meager living. At age 13, he heard the Dead Kennedys, and he knew he had to change the world. He went to protest the Mushroom WTO's oppression of Dinos and Cavemen in Dino Land, where he got in a fight with Koopa Troopa riot cops. He broke out of jail and went to fight at Land's End.
Mario Games that this Character would fit in (Made up games or Real games): Super Mario Politics, Super Mario RPG(couldn't anybody?), Yoshi 64
